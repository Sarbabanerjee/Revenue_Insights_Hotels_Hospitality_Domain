# Revenue_Insights_Hotels_Hospitality_Domain
• Elite Stays & Resorts noticed a loss in their market share and revenue over a few months. To understand the cause of  
this loss, they needed a way to analyze this. I modelled a dashboard in Power BI using three months of data.  
• With the constructed dashboard, Revenue team of Elite Stays & Resorts were able to gain insights about their revenue  
trend. This could help in regaining their revenue and market share by 20% in the next month.
